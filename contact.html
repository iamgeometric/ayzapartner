Toggle FAQ

Can I have things that aren't in the inventory?                                 

The inventory is built to have the most common items in merchandising readily available for you to view. You can request close to anything, and we'll try out best to add that to your campaign.

I can't find my contract. How do I get a new copy?

We, unfortunately, can't display your contract through the Partner Portal, but are more than willing to send you over a fresh copy upon request through email.

Are all partnerships secured under contract?

Yes. We want to make sure everyone gets what they're supposed here, so contracts are a necessity for precautionary measures. Most contracts are the same for all clients, but we are always open to negotiating parts if you are ever unhappy with it.

Can I work with Ayza Clothing beyond just preorder campaigns?

We're currently working around our pre-order campaign model exclusively. Though this is true for the moment, that doesn't mean we aren't open to the opportunity in the future.

How much money can I make with Ayza Clothing?

It is impossible to say unless you've done merchandising before in the past. The way your audience purchases and how much is does can vary widely. We often use a 1% model using social media followers, however even this can be widely off from what is actually made off of a campaign.
